**PiSugar 3 Plus** is the third generation of PiSugar Raspberry Pi UPS battery series, making Raspberry Pi a portable device. With independent MCU, on-board RTC, the Raspberry Pi can get the correct time whether it‘s powered or networked.

---
**PiSugar 3 Plus (5000mAh)** is essentially the **larger-capacity counterpart of PiSugar 3 (1200mAh)**, offering the same UPS functionality, I²C-based power management, RTC, and software features, but designed for **full-size Raspberry Pi boards** rather than the Zero series.

## Electrical Specifications
- **Input:** 5V-3Amax
- **Output:** 5V-3Amax
- **Battery capacity:** 5000 mAh
- **Communication interface:** I2C interface 0x57/0x68 address (can be set)

## Package contains
- 1 x battery module (with 5000mAh battery)
- 4 x screws

## Support Pi Models
- **Raspberry Pi 5B**
- **Raspberry Pi 4B**
- **Raspberry Pi 3B/3B+/3A+**
